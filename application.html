Overview

Real Flight Simulator is a realistic game that gives you the ultimate flight experience. There is the possibility to choose the aircraft you want and start to control the plane. You will become an expert in controlling the planes, reaching the heights and exploring skies.

You will be able to test the following aircrafts:

  • A-10
  • A-29
  • F-35
  • KC-10
  • M-262

How To Play

  1. Turn on the aircraft lights (Press Z) - optional, may cause bit lag issues.

    2. Keyboard Controls

    Note: The controls may vary depending on the aircraft you choose.

  2. Start-up the engine and wait for the startup sequence to complete (Press C).
  3. Throttle up the engines (Press and hold R or V to increase engine power)
  4. Release parking brakes (Press B)
  5. Rotate once takeoff speed is reached - above 150 knots (Press Down Arrow)
  6. Raise landing gear above 100ft (Press M)
  7. Continue with your flight...

    8. Real Flight Simulator Controls

    • AD to roll
    • WS to pitch
    • QE to yaw
    • RF for Engine Throttle (Power)
    • 1 to change weapon
    • 2 to lock target
    • Space to fire
    • Escape or F2 to main menu
    • H to hide controls info
