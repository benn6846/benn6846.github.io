ℹ️ About Campus Marketplace

Built by students, for students.

Our Mission

Campus Marketplace makes it easy for college students to safely buy and sell textbooks, dorm items, and essentials locally. No shipping, no scams — just real students helping each other out.

Who We Are

This project was started by students at the University of Northern Colorado to support our campus community. We're passionate about making college life more affordable and sustainable.

Join Us!

Whether you're looking to buy, sell, or just learn more, we invite you to explore Campus Marketplace. Together, we can make college life a little easier.
